About KrishnAI

KrishnAI is an AI-powered student guidance platform developed as an MCA minor project. It helps students deal with academic stress, confusion, and lack of motivation by providing thoughtful guidance through a Krishna-inspired AI chatbot.

Purpose of the System

The main objective of KrishnAI is to support students emotionally and academically by offering guidance inspired by timeless philosophical wisdom, explained in a simple and modern manner using Artificial Intelligence.

Application Modules

  • Dashboard: Central navigation hub — access all features after login.
  • Chat with Krishna: Interact with the AI chatbot for guidance on stress, fear, and motivation.
  • Daily Wisdom: A Bhagavad Gita shloka each day with meaning and student-friendly explanation.
  • Reflections: Write and store personal reflections to track your inner growth.

Technologies Used

  • Python & Flask
  • Groq AI (LLaMA 3.1)
  • SQLite Database
  • Flask-Login for Authentication
  • HTML & CSS (Cosmic Design System)
