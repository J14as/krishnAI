About KrishnAI

KrishnAI is an AI-powered student guidance platform developed as an MCA minor project. It helps students deal with academic stress, confusion, and lack of motivation by providing thoughtful guidance through a Krishna-inspired AI chatbot.

Purpose of the System

The main objective of KrishnAI is to support students emotionally and academically by offering guidance inspired by timeless philosophical wisdom, explained in a simple and modern manner using Artificial Intelligence.

Application Modules

  • Dashboard: Acts as the central navigation hub where users can access all features of the application after login.
  • Chat with Krishna: Allows students to interact with the AI chatbot to seek guidance related to stress, fear, confusion, motivation, and academic challenges.
  • Daily Wisdom: Displays a Bhagavad Gita shloka each day along with its meaning and a student-friendly explanation to provide daily motivation and clarity.
  • Reflections: Enables students to write and store personal reflections, helping them track thoughts, emotions, and personal growth over time.

Technologies Used

  • Python & Flask
  • Groq AI (LLaMA 3.1)
  • SQLite Database
  • Flask-Login for Authentication
  • HTML & CSS

Project Slogan

Ancient Wisdom. Modern Guidance.
